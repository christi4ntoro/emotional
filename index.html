Emotional Design

In this survey, you will be shown a selection of advertisements. For each advertisement, you will be asked to indicate your feelings using two scales:

  • Low-Arousal/High-Arousal Scale: Rate your arousal level while viewing the advertisement.
  • Unpleasant/Pleasant Scale: Rate how unpleasant or pleasant you felt while viewing the advertisement.


Additionally, if you recognize an advertisement, please indicate that you are familiar with it in the survey.

Your responses will provide valuable insights into emotional design and its impact on advertising.

Confidentiality

Your participation in this survey is voluntary, and all responses will remain anonymous. Personal information will not be shared with anyone. The data collected will only be used for academic purposes and will not be used for any commercial or marketing activities.
